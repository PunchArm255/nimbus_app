Nimbus is a modern, elegant personal stats tracker inspired by RPG games. It allows users to manage their personal stats, export shareable social media posts, and enjoy a fully customizable dark mode experience.

## Features

- **Stat Management**: Track and manage 5 main stats (Social, Physical, Lifestyle, Cognitive, and Emotional) with sub-stats to personalize your progress.
- **Social Media Export**: Create beautiful, shareable posts highlighting your stats.
- **Dark Mode**: Toggle a smooth, app-wide dark mode for a personalized user experience.
- **Authentication**: Sign up, sign in, and manage your account securely.
- **Debug Mode**: Explore the app without signing in (dev only).
- **Search & Friends**: Find and follow friends to compare stats and interact.
- **Streaks and Badges**: Gamify your personal growth with streaks and badges.
